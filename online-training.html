Online Training

Expert coaching and personalized programs delivered remotely to fit your lifestyle

Professional Coaching, Anywhere You Are

Our Online Training program brings the expertise of Desert Sports Med's coaches directly to you, no matter where you are. We combine personalized programming, video coaching, and ongoing support to deliver a comprehensive training experience that fits your schedule and lifestyle.

Unlike generic workout apps or one-size-fits-all online programs, our approach is fully customized to your specific goals, needs, and available equipment. We take the time to understand your unique situation and create a program that works for you, whether you're training at home, in a hotel gym, or at a commercial facility.

With our Online Training program, you'll receive the same level of expertise and personalization as our in-person clients, with the added flexibility of training on your own schedule. It's perfect for busy professionals, frequent travelers, or anyone who prefers the convenience of training on their own terms.

Personalized Programming

Video Coaching

How It Works

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive video consultation to understand your goals, training history, available equipment, schedule, and any limitations or injuries. This allows us to create a program that's perfectly tailored to your needs.

2

Movement Assessment

Through video analysis, we assess your movement patterns and exercise technique to identify any areas that need attention. This helps us design a program that addresses your specific needs and ensures safe, effective training.

3

Program Design

Based on your assessment and goals, we create a personalized training program delivered through our easy-to-use app. Your program includes detailed exercise descriptions, video demonstrations, and specific sets, reps, and loading parameters.

4

Regular Check-ins

You'll have regular video check-ins with your coach to review your progress, address any questions or concerns, and make adjustments to your program as needed. These sessions ensure you stay on track and continue making progress.

5

Ongoing Support

Between check-ins, you'll have unlimited messaging access to your coach through our app. Whether you have questions about exercises, need to adjust your program due to schedule changes, or want feedback on your form, we're here to help.

Program Features

Customized Workout Plans

Receive personalized training programs designed specifically for your goals, available equipment, and schedule. Your program will be updated regularly based on your progress and feedback.

User-Friendly App

Access your workouts, track your progress, and communicate with your coach through our intuitive, easy-to-use mobile app. Available for both iOS and Android devices.

Exercise Video Library

Learn proper technique with access to our extensive library of exercise demonstration videos, ensuring you perform each movement safely and effectively.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your progress with detailed tracking of workouts, measurements, and performance metrics. Visualize your improvements over time to stay motivated.

Flexible Scheduling

Train on your own schedule with workouts designed to fit your lifestyle and time constraints. No need to adhere to specific class times or appointment slots.

Nutrition Guidance

Receive basic nutrition recommendations to support your training goals, including meal timing, portion control, and food quality guidelines.
