Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to receive treatment?

No, you do not need a referral to receive treatment at Desert Sports Med. We offer direct access to our services, allowing you to schedule an appointment without a physician referral. However, some insurance plans may require a referral for coverage, so we recommend checking with your insurance provider.

How long does a typical treatment session last?

Initial assessments typically last 60-90 minutes, while follow-up treatment sessions are usually 45-60 minutes. The duration may vary depending on the specific service and your individual needs.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. Coverage varies depending on your specific plan and the services you need. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to verify coverage for physical therapy and sports medicine services. We're also happy to help you understand your benefits - just give us a call.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions needed varies depending on your specific condition, goals, and how your body responds to treatment. After your initial assessment, we'll provide a recommended treatment plan with an estimated number of sessions. We regularly reassess your progress and adjust the plan as needed.

What should I wear to my appointment?

We recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows for movement and provides access to the area being treated. Athletic wear such as t-shirts, shorts, or leggings is ideal. For lower extremity issues, shorts are recommended. For upper extremity or spine issues, a tank top or t-shirt is appropriate.
