Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment in several ways:

  • Use our online booking system on the Book Online page
  • Call us at (801) 797-4043
  • Email us at desertsportsmed@gmail.com
  • Visit our facility in person

What should I bring to my first appointment?

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Photo ID
  • Insurance card (if applicable)
  • Any relevant medical records or imaging reports
  • A list of current medications
  • Comfortable clothing appropriate for movement and assessment
  • Payment method for any co-pays or self-pay services

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers for eligible services, including physical therapy and certain sports medicine treatments. Coverage varies depending on your specific plan and the services you need. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to verify coverage. We're also happy to help you understand your benefits - just give us a call.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that schedules can change. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please do so at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation fee. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a fee of 50% of the service price. No-shows may be charged the full price of the service.

How long will my treatment take?

The duration of treatment varies depending on your specific condition, goals, and how your body responds to treatment. After your initial assessment, we'll provide a recommended treatment plan with an estimated timeline. We regularly reassess your progress and adjust the plan as needed. Some conditions may resolve in a few sessions, while others may require ongoing care.
