Semi-Private Training

Personalized coaching in a small group setting for maximum results

The Perfect Balance of Personal Attention and Group Motivation

Semi-Private Training at Desert Sports Med offers the perfect balance between personalized coaching and the motivational benefits of training with others. In small groups of 2-4 people, you'll receive individualized attention from our expert coaches while enjoying the camaraderie and friendly competition that comes with group training.

Unlike large group fitness classes where it's easy to get lost in the crowd, our Semi-Private Training ensures that your form is monitored, your program is tailored to your specific needs, and your progress is tracked consistently. At the same time, you'll benefit from the energy, accountability, and cost-effectiveness of training with others.

Whether you're looking to improve your strength, lose weight, enhance athletic performance, or simply develop a healthier lifestyle, our Semi-Private Training program provides the structure, guidance, and support you need to achieve your goals.

Small Group Setting

Train in groups of 2-4 people with similar goals and fitness levels for a personalized yet social experience.

Individualized Programming

Receive a customized training program designed specifically for your goals, needs, and fitness level.

Cost-Effective

Enjoy the benefits of personalized coaching at a fraction of the cost of one-on-one personal training.

How It Works

1

Initial Assessment

We begin with a comprehensive assessment to understand your current fitness level, movement patterns, goals, and any limitations or injuries. This allows us to create a personalized training program that's right for you.

2

Group Matching

Based on your assessment, goals, and schedule, we'll match you with a small group of 1-3 other individuals with similar objectives and fitness levels. This ensures that your training environment is both supportive and appropriately challenging.

3

Customized Programming

Our coaches develop individualized training programs for each member of the group. While you'll be training alongside others, your exercises, sets, reps, and loads will be specifically tailored to your needs and goals.

4

Scheduled Sessions

You'll attend scheduled training sessions with your group and coach. Most clients train 2-3 times per week, but we can accommodate different frequencies based on your goals and availability.

5

Progress Tracking

We regularly track your progress through assessments, measurements, and performance metrics. Your program will be adjusted based on your progress to ensure you continue to move toward your goals.
